3) Yu Yu hakusho

Yu Yu Hakusho follows Yusuke Urameshi, a 14-year-old street-brawling delinquent who, in an uncharacteristic act of altruism, is hit by a car and killed in an attempt to save a young boy by pushing him out of the way. ... Yusuke succeeds with the help of his friends Keiko Yukimura and Kazuma Kuwabara.
